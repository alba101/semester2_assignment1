Welcome to the Bookmic

A bookstore and cafe under one roof! Grab a book and a coffee and relax, or visit us on one of our event days to see performances, open mics, book signings and more.

We are an independent bookstore/ coffee shop.
Situated at the heart of Bristol city.
Bringing you the coldest coffees with the warmest service.

Our stage

The stage is open to many events and we love to host them. We welcome local talents to to perform and represent this beautiful city.

Our cafe

The cafe is filled with delicious drinks and delectable treats. All foods and drinks are made in house, fresh every day.

Our bookstore

The bookstore has a wide range of genres for everyone to enjoy. Once you've picked one out how about you sit down with a coffee and strart reading it straight away?

Also Available

-We have a brand new art supplies aisle with oil paints, canvases, watercolours and more.

-Vegan and vegetarian alternatives are available with no extra charge!

-Board games are also available to rent in house so you can enjoy your family favourites whilst sipping on your delicious brew.
